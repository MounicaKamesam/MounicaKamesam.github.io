SheHacks Boston is a large collaborative effort on behalf of women from universities in the Greater Boston area to host the largest all-female hackathon in the world. Its mission is to empower women in computer science and provide them with opportunities to explore the tech industry in an energizing environment and create an inclusive community. For 36 hours, 1,000+ developers, designers, and builders from high schools and colleges around the world will work together to build applications, websites, and hardware projects.
